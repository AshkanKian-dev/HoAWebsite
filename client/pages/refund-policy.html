Refund Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. All Sales Are Final

All purchases through Heart of Acheron are for digital goods (in-game items, perks, and server benefits). Because digital goods are delivered instantly and cannot be returned, all sales are final and non-refundable once the item has been successfully delivered to your in-game character.

By completing a purchase you confirm that you understand and accept this policy.

2. When Refunds May Be Issued

A refund will be considered only in the following situations:

  • Delivery failure — your item was not delivered to your character and we are unable to resolve the issue within 72 hours of your purchase
  • Duplicate charge — you were charged more than once for the same order due to a technical error on our side
  • Unauthorised transaction — you did not make the purchase and can demonstrate this

Refunds are not issued for:

  • Change of mind after delivery
  • Account bans resulting from rule violations
  • Server downtime or resets (unless caused by a confirmed technical failure on our part)
  • Items lost due to in-game mechanics (death, decay, etc.)

3. How to Request a Refund

If you believe you qualify for a refund, contact us within 7 days of your purchase with:

  • Your order ID (found in your confirmation email or order tracking page)
  • Your email address or Steam ID used at checkout
  • A brief description of the issue

We will respond within 3 business days. Approved refunds are processed back to your original payment method and typically appear within 5–10 business days depending on your bank or PayPal.

4. Chargebacks

Filing a chargeback or payment dispute for a successfully delivered order is considered a violation of our Terms of Service and will result in a permanent account ban. Please contact us first — we are happy to resolve genuine issues directly.

5. Contact

Refund requests and questions: use our contact form and mark the subject "Refund Request".
