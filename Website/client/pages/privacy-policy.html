Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Who We Are

Heart of Acheron operates a private Conan Exiles game server and online store. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how long we keep it, and your rights regarding that data.

2. What Data We Collect

When you register an account:

  • Email address
  • Display name / character name
  • Steam ID (if you log in via Steam)
  • Steam avatar (fetched from Steam's public API)
  • Account creation date

When you make a purchase:

  • Email address
  • Steam ID and in-game character name (for item delivery)
  • Order amount, product purchased, and timestamp
  • Payment provider used (Stripe or PayPal) and transaction reference

What we do NOT collect:

  • Credit card numbers or bank details — all payment data is handled directly by Stripe or PayPal and never passes through our servers
  • Your Steam password — Steam login uses OpenID, which means you authenticate on Steam's own website and we only receive your public SteamID

Automatically collected data:

  • IP address — stored temporarily in login attempt logs for security (brute-force protection)
  • Browser user-agent — stored in session records to detect session hijacking
  • Server access logs (standard web server logs, retained for 30 days)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account
  • To deliver purchased in-game items via RCON commands to the game server
  • To send order confirmation and delivery notification emails
  • To enforce server rules and prevent abuse
  • To respond to support requests

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Stripe — processes credit/debit card payments. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • PayPal — processes PayPal payments. Subject to PayPal's Privacy Policy.
  • Steam / Valve — provides OpenID authentication and public profile data. Subject to Steam's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data — retained until you request deletion or your account is terminated
  • Purchase/order records — retained for 7 years for financial record-keeping requirements, but your personal identifiers may be anonymised on request
  • Session tokens — expire after 7–30 days and are purged automatically
  • Login attempt logs — purged after 30 days
  • Server access logs — retained for 30 days

6. Your Rights (GDPR & General)

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and personal data. We will complete deletion within 30 days. Note: anonymised order records may be retained for legal compliance.
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us here. You may also delete your account directly from your profile page.

7. Security

We protect your data with HTTPS encryption in transit, bcrypt password hashing, rate limiting on all login endpoints, session token validation, and CSRF protection on all state-changing requests. We never store raw payment card data.

8. Cookies

We use only essential cookies — specifically a session cookie used for the Steam OpenID login flow. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

9. Contact

Privacy questions or data requests: use our contact form and mark the subject "Privacy Request".
